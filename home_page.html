ROOTS YOGA VEDANTA CENTRES & ASHRAMS, DHARAMKOT


Experience the classical teachings of yoga and vedanta in the country of its birth. The Roots Yoga Vedanta Centres & Ashrams has locations throughout India and is dedicated to promoting physical, mental and spiritual health through the teachings of yoga.

Our founder Swami Vishnudevananda synthesised the essence of yoga into five principles that could be easily incorporated into modern daily life.

Our network of Ashrams and City Centres offer teachings on all aspects of yoga and meditation including:

  • Yoga Vacation Programme including Raja Yoga and Roots
  • Teachers’ Training Course
  • Continued Education
  • Karma Yoga Opportunities

Join us in your journey of self-discovery in any of our City Centres and Ashrams!
